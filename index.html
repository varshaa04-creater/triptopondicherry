These were the best days of this year

I actually found this place by accident. Family members were busy planning a wedding, so I went for a walk. Soon I saw signs indicating a beach and decided to check it out. When I got there, the beach was pretty much empty. It was wide, long, and very clean. The sand is warm and there was no pollution of any kind. I had no luck finding shells or much sea life, but that did not diminish its beauty. After a couple of hours that seems like eternity (in a good way!) I went back to our hotel. The next day I brought my family down there and our young children loved playing in the warm water. I live in a city with beautiful beaches, but this one stands out in my memories
